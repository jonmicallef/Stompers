Stompers Pride

Stompers History

Stompers Rugby Club was first established in 1995. The initial years posed challenges and the club was more or less wound up a couple of years later, only to be reformed in 1999, proving resilience and tenacity from the get-go. Just two years following this resurgence, in 2001, we clinched our first trophy. This victory cemented our legacy and introduced the Bulldog Bark that will forever echo through Maltese rugby. Since then, Stompers has maintained a formidable presence in Malta Rugby.

The Stompers Experience

Joining Stompers means becoming part of a storied tradition and embracing the values that define rugby. Whether you're a seasoned player looking for a new challenge or a beginner eager to learn the ropes, we welcome everyone with open arms. Our experienced coaches and supportive teammates will help you develop your talents, pushing you to reach your full potential.

Malta Rugby Trophy Case

Ray Elliot - Champions Cup

This Trophy is the season opener played in honour of one of Malta's first Coaches Ray Elliot. It is played by the winner of the league and the winner of the cup.

Cisk - League

Sponsored by - Cisk

Cisk League is a 2-round nail biting League. It spans the length of the whole season. Seeing almost 15 games played. Sponsored and endorsed by Cisk.

Knock-Out - Cup

The cup is a cut throat knock out Cup. Lose a game and you go home empty handed. Every Club battles it out for the last piece of silverware for the season.

Women League

The newly added women 7s series which Stompers successfully secured the first trophy in 2022/23 Women's league, 2 years after formation.
